~$ Advent of Cyber 2022

Posted on Dec. 1st, 2022. | Est. reading time: 2 minutes

Every year since 2019, TryHackMe has been hosting an event called Advent Of Cyber, which - much like the Advent of Code event it is inspired by - takes place from the 1st to the 24th of December.

2022 marked the fourth installment of this event, located here on TryHackMe.

Each "day" has been split off in a separate walkthrough, for legibility's sake. Also, you may want to preserve your scroll finger.
