~$ Reading Materials!

Posted on Nov. 12th, 2020. | Est. reading time: 1 minutes

Tags:InfoSecPersonal


One of the things I've been trying to get better at in these late months of 2020, is the litterature surrounding processes and the nomenclature of CyberSec.

To that end, I've started reading some books!

Upon the recommendations of TJ Null I've started reading two books related to InfoSec.

Currently, I'm on "Penetration Testing: A Hands-On Introduction to Hacking (1st Edition)" by Georgia Weidman, which is a pretty cool introduction to the field and it's concepts, although some of the procedures are a bit dated.


My next reading project will be "Attacking Network Protocols: A Hacker's Guide to Capture, Analysis, and Exploitation" by James Forshaw, which promises to be an interesting read as well.
