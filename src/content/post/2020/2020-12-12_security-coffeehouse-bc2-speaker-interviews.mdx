A short while back, I spoke about IoT at BeerCon2 (see related blogpost <LocalLink text="here" url="/post/2020/2020_11_02+I-gave-a-talk-at-BeerCon2" />). I thought at that point that that would be the last I'd hear of that event for a short while. Thankfully, <TwitterLink text="James Bore" url="https://twitter.com/Coffee_Fueled" /> had other ideas.

At the Security CoffeeHouse, a panel-esque platform he created, he had decided to invite the BeerCon2 speakers over in small groups to talk about the event, in an informal setting.

The first session was with <TwitterLink text="Ayush" url="https://twitter.com/ayushpriya10" />, <TwitterLink text="Elizabeth" url="https://twitter.com/ElizabethMomola" />, <TwitterLink text="Lennaert" url="https://twitter.com/lennaert89" /> and myself.

We discussed the steps that brought up our participation, how we prepared, what we thought about it, and the like.
